The people around you are the people you care the most about: your family and friends.

Whether you are at the pub, a concert, or family dinner, we believe it should be really easy to share your photos, videos and files with the people around you.

That's why we develop PortalShare.

With PortalShare, you don't need to setup WiFi networks or mess with Bluetooth connections.

All you need to do is set a key phrase for the content you want to share, and your friends can access the content by entering the phrase. Zero setup time, so you can focus on the experience, not the technology.

Sharing with PortalShare is secure. Your data is transferred over an encrypted connection. And of course it is available only to those people that you give the key phrase to.

We value your privacy. PortalShare does not collect your private information, and you are not required to sign up.
