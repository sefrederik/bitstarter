SuperSplit

A social and simple way to split the bill

Want to know when SuperSplit is ready?

We always found that splitting the bill after a great evening out with friends was a cumbersome experience. Everyone had a different dish, some had one drink while others had three, and a few of us also had coffee aftwards. And then there was a 10% discount. It won't be long until some of us fire up a calculator app and realize that the allegedly simple math for splitting a bill is quite challening after three beers. Sounds familiar?

Splitting the bill should be simple. You want to spent time with your friends, not with a calculator app. That's why we develop SuperSplit. SuperSplit changes splitting the bill from awkward into a social experience. You just snap a photo of the bill and it is shared automatically with your friends around you. Everyone simply taps on the items they had. SuperSplit shows who owes what — including a tip if you want.

You immediately see which items your friends have claimed, making it fast and easy to split a bill. And of course, SuperSplit also allows you to divvy an item (think shared salads) or consider discounts.

SuperSplit is most fun if everyone in your party uses it. That's why we have decided to make SuperSplit available for free. In that way, you and your friends can try it without having to cut into your beer money. Cheers!
